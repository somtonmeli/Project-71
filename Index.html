School Accomplishments

School Environmental Pollution Presentation Competition
Our school won first in the Informative Sector and sencond in the Presentation Sector

The task was to create a presentation that show how human are affecting the health of the planet we live on Earth, and how there are currently environmental crisis all over the world, they were allowed to choose how to present the Powerpoint but they had to make the presentation in their own way.

Inter Class Science Project Competition
Several students won prizes such as the most creative and innovative aswell as the prize for creating the most intricately designed project

Students were given the task of creating a science project on the topic of their choice as long as they would be able to make a detailed project out of it.The winners recieved prizes such as certificates and medal depending on how well they did.

Inter Class Abstract Art Contest
We told our students to create an abstract art piece but not any painting the painting, drawing or even digitised art has to have a hidden meaning behind it.

STudents come up with most fasinating ideas when you give them the creative space and it was a delight to see the smiles on thier faces as each and everyone of them created the materpiece that has a meaning of significance to them.
